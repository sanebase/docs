title: Instant Access

Instant Access is quite simply an enriched alternative to directly accessing Stripe's API.

- ✅ Instant access to live Stripe data
- ✅ No rate limits
- ✅ No throttling
- ✅ No need to wait for webhooks
- ✅ No need to maintain state in your database &ndash; treat it like your own micro-service
